📊 Vrinda Store – Sales Annual Report Dashboard (2022)

Excited to share an Excel dashboard I created to analyze Vrinda Store’s 2022 sales performance using data visualization and insights-driven reporting.

This dashboard highlights key business metrics in a simple and interactive way:

🔹 Orders vs Sales Trend (Monthly) – Clear view of how orders and revenue moved throughout the year

 🔹 Customer Insights – Women contributed 64% of total sales, showing a strong target segment
 
 🔹 Top Performing Channels – Amazon and Flipkart lead the sales channels significantly
 
 🔹 Top 5 States by Sales – Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are major revenue drivers
 
 🔹 Order Status Analysis – 92% orders successfully delivered, indicating strong fulfillment performance
 
 🔹 Age vs Gender Orders – Adult women are the highest purchasing group
 
 🔹 Interactive Filters – Month, Channel, and Category slicers for dynamic analysis
